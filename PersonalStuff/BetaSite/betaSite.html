Scrambled Eggs Recipe

Source: TheIncredibleEgg.com

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
    • White Eggs
  • 1/4 Cup Milk
  • 2 tsp. butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. BEAT eggs, milk, salt and pepper in medium bowl until blended.
  2. HEAT butter in large nonstick skillet over medium heat until hot.
    POUR IN egg mixture. As eggs begin to set, GENTLY PULL the eggs
    across the pan with a spatula, forming large soft curds.
  3. CONTINUE cooking – pulling, lifting and folding eggs – until thickened
    and no visible liquid egg remains. Do not stir constantly. REMOVE
    from heat. SERVE immediately.

Definitions

Egg
the contents of the egg of a bird and especially a chicken that is eaten as food.
Milk
an opaque white fluid rich in fat and protein.
