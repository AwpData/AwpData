Whale Shark

A whale shark (Rhincodon Typus) is considered one of the largest fish in the world!
They love warm climates which is why they live in tropical oceans around Central America and South America.
Whale sharks can live up to 100 years old!

FUN FACT: Whale sharks feed on plankton as their throats are the size of quarters!
